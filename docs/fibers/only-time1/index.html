ONLY TIME MAKES IT HUMAN I

Since March 2020, I've been collecting the plastic bags from grocery stores and the plastic waste from packaging because of my finding that everyday thin plastics cannot be curbside recycled. This knowledge sent me on a spiral to just hoard all the plastic I can find and see what I can do to repurpose it. Through my material exploration and experimentation, I found ways to turn these plastics into rope. This was the beginning of my wanting to weave with plastic. I've translated the ideas and techniques of traditional weaving to plastic. With this, I've learned new ways of manipulating plastic to create texture and shapes.

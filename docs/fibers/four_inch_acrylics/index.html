FINGERING MYSELF WITH FOUR INCH ACRYLICS ON

I'm trying to help myself talk about my sexuality. Intimacy is really hard for me. Sometimes sensations like that are really hard. But for some reason, I yearn for them.

I wanted to explore language and text through a tapestry. I think it’s really intense when you are being spoken to. I want to be loud and passionate and sexual and gay and real. I love how loud I’m being. I screamed through the treads.

The nails are so important. This pillow isn’t just about sex. It’s about femininity and touch and heat and hurt and grief and hatred and self.

The pillow is so soft. The nails are so sharp.

It hurts.

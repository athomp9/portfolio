HELLO!

My name is Arthur Thompson (they/them), I use the name ArthurArthur as an alias to remove myself from the societal structure of naming. Though my birth name is Arthur, I understand my name as Arthur and nothing else. As of right now, I am focused on the body as material, and of course plastic! I love playing with physicality. I also love design work, typography, image-making, and exploring the limitlessness of my identity. Currently, I am a student exploring visual design, sculpture, fibers, and performance at The School of the Art Institute of Chicago. Send me a message to my email and say HI!

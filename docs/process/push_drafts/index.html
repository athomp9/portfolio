Drafts of puSH~

puSH~ is a lot of things, one of them being a whole and overwhelming emphasis on the perception and aesthetics of my body. So it would only make sense that there are images that I didn’t accept to be good enough. These are drafts from the original series—photos that I didn’t think fit each part as I made and explored ideas.

Enjoy the flowing, lyrical visions of my body. The images are laid out in order of production so that you can see the evolution of my experimentation.

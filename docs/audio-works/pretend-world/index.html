PRETEND WORLD

When I am on the train late at night, I can feel myself becoming a part of the train. In those moments, there is no distinction between myself and the train. Pretend World is a soundscape of this concept—the swift motion, the tug back and forth, the lack of control—an auditory ascension and descension.

I created elements of this ensemble with an electronic modular synth, and others found sounds. The video was produced using a visualizer that reacts to the audio. Soundspectrum developed this technology.

This piece was featured in the My Dreams These Days exhibition—a 12-hour showcase of student artist work streamed consecutively online as well as projected live at physical locations in the Chicago area.

Featured in the My Dreams These Days virtual gallery in June 2021 and in the curatorial collaboration with New Art City.

2021, audio and video performance, 13:00, recorded and found sounds

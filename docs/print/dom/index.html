DOMINATION; submission

This print explores and visualizes two words, Domination and Submission. Domination is placed symmetrically in the center, whereas Submission is asymmetric. I wanted to conceptualize these to make Domination power over Submission. I used the ornament of a capital ‘O’ to simulate a sort of wrap or tie around the word Submission within the ‘O’’s counter space. I want to put these ideas in the context of sexual taboos. What is said quietly or never said at all.

2021, Screenprint, 11”x 15”

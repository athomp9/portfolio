I AM PRETENDING; YOU ARE PRETENDING; ANYFORM; ANYSHAPE; ANYTHING.

I experienced an overwhelming sense of imposter syndrome when producing this edition, completely doubting my abilities and practice. I got so caught up in the broken routine that I felt like I was pretending to be an artist. It felt as my practice didn't mean anything to anyone or myself. It was complicated. I found the only way to get me out of this headspace was to process this through a print. Screaming it through this edition was my way out.

This composition features a repetition of the phrases: "I am pretending, You are pretending, any form, any shape, anything."

2021, Lithograph, 15”x 21”, Edition of 8

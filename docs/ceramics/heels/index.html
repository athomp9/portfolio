HEELS

This project started as a way to manipulate the way I can look and how I can contort my body. I wanted platform high heels that pointed my body forwards.

The process of making shifted my thinking. I originally made the size of these shoes to fit me. They then shrunk, leaving me with a pair of shoes that didn’t fit me. 

I wanted to be able to find shoes that fit me in the styles I liked. So I made shoes that fit me. But ironically, they still don’t. 

I’m finding this to be kind of heartbreaking. Can’t I even make for my own body? It made me realize how much work I have left to do. I want to be able to make something for me.

2022, 8 inch ceramic heels.

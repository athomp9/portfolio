MY PENIS PURSE

A purse shaped like a penis with a pearl beaded strap.

At first, this project was a joke, but it pivoted quickly into an accessory that I hold so much meaning in. I've been figuring out how I need myself to feel. It isn't easy, and I wanted an object to tell this story for me. It's the heaviest purse you will ever hold, and it's an exaggerated penis. The purse's strap is made of plastic pearl beads that roll right off your shoulder. It's the most exhaustive and inconvenient object.

But also, haha, penis.

2022, ceramic, plastic pearls, eye screws, wire.

13 inches long.
